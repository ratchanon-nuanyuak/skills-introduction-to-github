About NewJeans

NewJeans is a K-pop girl group under ADOR, a subsidiary of HYBE. Known for their fresh concepts and catchy songs, they are redefining the K-pop scene.

Debut Date: July 22, 2022
Label: ADOR (HYBE Labels)

Members

  • Minji: Leader, Vocalist
  • Hanni: Main Vocalist, Dancer
  • Danielle: Lead Dancer, Vocalist
  • Haerin: Vocalist
  • Hyein: Maknae, Vocalist

Popular Tracks

Some of their hit songs include:

  • Attention - A fresh and catchy debut song
  • Hype Boy - Showcasing their diverse vocals
  • Cookie - A groovy, sweet tune
  • Ditto - A ballad with heartfelt lyrics

Achievements

NewJeans has won numerous awards, including Best New Artist and several music show wins within their debut year. Their influence continues to grow internationally.

Follow their journey as they bring fresh energy to the K-pop industry!
